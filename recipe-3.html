Hamburger Pie

Ingredients

  • 1 pound ground beef
  • Potato Buds instant puffs (enough for 8 servings)
  • 1 egg
  • 1 teaspoon salt
  • 1/8 teaspoon pepper
  • 1 tablespoon instant minced onion
  • 3/4 cup catsup
  • 1 cup milk
  • 1/2 cup shredded sharp Cheddar cheese (2 ounces)

Preparation

  1. Heat oven to 350°.

  2. Mix meat, 1 1/3 cups of the instant puffs (dry), the egg, salt, pepper, onion, catsup and milk.

  3. Spread in ungreased pie pan, 9×1 1/2 inches. Bake uncovered 35 to 40 minutes.

  4. Prepare remaining instant puffs as directed on package for 4 servings.

  5. Top baked meat loaf with mashed potatoes; sprinkle with cheese.

  6. Bake 3 to 4 minutes longer or until cheese melts. 4 or 5 servings.
